## ğŸ¯ How to Play

1. Press **SPACE** or **UP ARROW** to start the game
2. Press **SPACE** or **UP ARROW** to make the bird flap and fly
3. Navigate through the gaps between pipes
4. Avoid hitting pipes or the ground
5. Try to beat your high score!

Press **ESC** to quit at any time.
